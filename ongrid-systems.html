Detailed Information: OnGrid Systems

Our OnGrid solar systems are designed to seamlessly integrate with your existing utility grid. This means you can generate your own electricity, reduce your reliance on traditional power sources, and even sell excess energy back to the grid, potentially earning credits on your electricity bill.

These systems are ideal for residential, commercial, and industrial properties that have access to the public grid. They offer a reliable and efficient way to transition to renewable energy without compromising on power availability, as the grid acts as a constant backup.

Key Benefits:

  • Reduced electricity bills through self-generation and net metering.
  • Reliable power supply with grid backup.
  • Contribution to a cleaner environment by reducing carbon emissions.
  • Long-term energy savings and increased property value.
  • Low maintenance requirements.

Components:

  • High-efficiency Solar Panels
  • Grid-Tie Inverter
  • Mounting Structure
  • Bi-directional Meter (for net metering)
  • Cabling and Safety Devices
