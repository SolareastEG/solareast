About Solareast EG

Solareast EG is a leading provider of comprehensive solar energy solutions in Egypt. With a commitment to sustainability and innovation, we specialize in designing, installing, and maintaining high-quality solar power systems for residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

Our mission is to accelerate the adoption of renewable energy, reduce carbon footprints, and empower communities with clean, affordable, and reliable electricity. We pride ourselves on our expert team, superior technology, and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction.

Our Founder: Osama Ellabban

Osama Ellabban has been a dedicated professional in the solar energy field in Egypt since 2008, a period when the solar market was in its nascent stages in the region. His extensive experience and commitment have been instrumental in shaping the renewable energy landscape.

He has played a pivotal role in numerous significant projects, demonstrating his profound expertise and leadership in large-scale solar installations across various industries.

Our Team: Ahmed Ellabban - CCO

Ahmed Ellabban serves as the Chief Commercial Officer (CCO) at Solareast EG. He is responsible for developing and implementing commercial strategies to drive growth, expand market presence, and foster strong client relationships.
